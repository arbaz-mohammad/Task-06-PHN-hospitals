Orthopedics

Overview

Orthopedics is a medical specialty focused on the diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of conditions and injuries related to the musculoskeletal system, which includes bones, muscles, joints, ligaments, and tendons. Orthopedic doctors, also known as orthopedic surgeons, provide care for a wide range of musculoskeletal problems, such as fractures, sprains, arthritis, back pain, sports injuries, and deformities. They utilize various surgical and non-surgical techniques to improve the function and mobility of the musculoskeletal system.

Services Offered

  • Fracture management
  • Joint replacement surgery
  • Sports medicine and injury treatment
  • Arthroscopy
  • Spine surgery
  • Orthopedic trauma care
  • Physical therapy and rehabilitation

Benefits of Orthopedics

  • Expert care for musculoskeletal conditions and injuries
  • Improved mobility and function
  • Relief from pain and discomfort
  • Treatment of sports-related injuries
  • Enhanced quality of life
  • Access to specialized surgical interventions
