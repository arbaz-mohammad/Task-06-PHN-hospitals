Psychiatry

Overview

Psychiatry is a medical specialty dedicated to the diagnosis, prevention, and treatment of mental disorders. It focuses on understanding the biological, psychological, and social factors that contribute to mental illnesses. Psychiatrists are medical doctors who assess, diagnose, and provide treatment for individuals experiencing mental health issues.

Services Offered

  • Psychiatric evaluations
  • Medication management
  • Individual therapy
  • Group therapy
  • Family therapy
  • Psychological testing
  • Substance abuse treatment

Benefits of Psychiatry

  • Effective management of mental health conditions
  • Improved quality of life
  • Enhanced coping skills
  • Support for recovery and resilience
  • Access to a comprehensive treatment approach
