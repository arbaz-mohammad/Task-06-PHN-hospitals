Pulmonology

Overview

Pulmonology is a medical specialty that focuses on the diagnosis, treatment, and management of diseases and conditions related to the respiratory system. Pulmonologists, also known as chest physicians, specialize in caring for patients with lung diseases, such as asthma, chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD), pneumonia, lung cancer, and sleep disorders like sleep apnea. They utilize various diagnostic techniques and treatment options to improve respiratory health and overall lung function.

Services Offered

  • Diagnosis and management of respiratory diseases
  • Pulmonary function testing
  • Chest X-rays and imaging studies
  • Bronchoscopy and other interventional procedures
  • Asthma and COPD treatment
  • Lung cancer screening and treatment
  • Sleep disorder evaluation and management

Benefits of Pulmonology

  • Expert care for respiratory conditions
  • Improved lung function and breathing
  • Diagnosis and treatment of lung diseases
  • Management of asthma, COPD, and other chronic conditions
  • Early detection and treatment of lung cancer
  • Effective management of sleep disorders
